  4. AIWargame, 2023.
    • Developed an adversarial asymmetric strategy game involving two distinct players (attacker and defender), features unique units such as AI, Viruses, Techs, Programs, and Firewalls on a 5x5 board. The objective is to strategically outmaneuver the opponent, focusing on the destruction of the opponent's AI unit.
    • Implemented an adversarial search algorithm to autonomously play the AI Wargame. This involved the integration of a minimax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning and the development of two unique heuristics (e1 and e2) to guide the AI's decision-making process. Ensured the AI operated efficiently across various modes including manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic, using a simple command-line interface for interactions.
  5. Pacman, 2022 - 2023.

      4. Application of Machine Learning techniques to carry out prediction and classification tasks in the Pac-Man using Weka.
